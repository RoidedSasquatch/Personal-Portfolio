About Me

Computer Programming/Analysis

Hello! My name is Dan, I am a programming/analysis student at Algonquin College in Ottawa, Canada. I am 26 years old and prior to beginning my program at Algonquin, I completed an honours degree in psychology. After gaining more experience in the workforce as a salesperson, I decided to revaluate my true passions and noticed that most of what I enjoy relates to computing. I took my first programming class in high school, I remember being amazed at what I could do through a few lines of code, despite the fact that we were using Visual Basic 6 - an older programming language. As such, I applied to the computer programming and analysis program at Algonquin and got accepted, and now find myself in my second year becoming increasingly grateful for all that I am learning. My short time in this program has only reinforced my desire to become a software developer and I have even on several personal java projects to further my learning. Ultimately, it is my dream to move to British Columbia with my girlfriend and start my own software development company - once I have some experience as a developer with existing companies. BC is a special place to me as it is the first place I travelled to solo.

Computer Programming and Analysis

Algonquin College is a well-known college located in the capital city of Canada, Ottawa. The Computer Programming and Analysis program at Algonquin is a highly competitve program that trains students to be knowledgable software developers and analysists. The program consists of six academic semesters and two co-op work terms. Students gain sought-after industry knowledge and become skilled in the use of Java, HTML, CSS, PHP, Javascript, Python, and SQL. Additionally, students are trained to use various software tools including several Database Management tools and VMware.
